About

Celebrating Love, Capturing Moments.
Your Wedding, Our Cinematic Story.

Here at EKO

Experience the magic of Dorset and Hampshire's premier husband and wife wedding videography team.

We specialise in capturing natural, authentic love stories across Dorset, Hampshire, and South England - preserving the genuine emotions and candid moments that make your day uniquely yours.

Based in Bournemouth, we bring over a decade of expertise in wedding videography to every celebration. As a married couple ourselves, we understand the emotions and significance of your wedding day. This personal connection allows us to anticipate those fleeting moments - the look between you during your vows, your parents' proud tears, the spontaneous laughter with friends - and capture them beautifully and discreetly.

Our Approach

Your Love Story, Naturally Told

We believe the best wedding films aren't staged or posed - they're captured as they naturally unfold.

Our approach is simple: we blend into your celebration, allowing you to be completely present with your loved ones whilst we document the authentic joy, emotion, and connection that makes your day special. The result? A cinematic film that truly feels like your wedding, not a performance.

The EKO Difference

Why Choose a Husband and Wife Team?

Working as a married couple gives us unique insight into wedding day emotions.

We've experienced our own wedding, so we know which moments matter most. Our seamless coordination means comprehensive coverage - whilst one of us captures your ceremony, the other is ready for those candid guest reactions and intimate family moments. We work together intuitively, ensuring nothing precious is missed whilst remaining completely unobtrusive.

Our Story

How it all started

We launched EKO Wedding Videography following our own wedding, when we realised how quickly the day passes and how precious those authentic moments become.

What began as a love for storytelling has grown into a dedicated service capturing genuine emotions and real connections. Every wedding reminds us why we're passionate about this work - it's about preserving the laughter, tears, joy, and love that make each celebration unique. These aren't just wedding films; they're family heirlooms that become more treasured with every anniversary.

Our Values

What makes us different

Natural & Unobtrusive

We don't direct or stage your day. Instead, we document it as it naturally unfolds, capturing candid moments that reveal the authentic emotions and connections that make your celebration special.

Personal Understanding

As a married couple, we bring genuine empathy to every wedding. We've experienced the nerves, joy, and overwhelming love of a wedding day, and this helps us anticipate and capture those meaningful moments others might miss.

Dual Perspective Coverage

Two videographers working in perfect harmony means comprehensive coverage without disruption. We're positioned to capture both the main action and the candid reactions, the grand moments and the quiet, intimate exchanges.

Dorset & Hampshire Specialists

Years of filming across both counties means we understand local venues, lighting conditions, and those perfect spots for natural couple portraits. We work seamlessly with your venue to ensure beautiful footage whilst respecting their guidelines.

Professional Quality

Equipped with professional cameras, audio equipment, and backup systems, we ensure every vow, speech, and heartfelt moment is captured with crystal-clear quality - ready to be relived for generations.

The Experience

Working with us

From our first conversation to delivering your finished film, we're dedicated to making the videography experience effortless and enjoyable.

We take time to understand your unique story, discuss your vision, and ensure you feel completely comfortable on camera (even if you think you're not photogenic - trust us, everyone is on their wedding day!).
